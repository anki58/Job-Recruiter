Job Description
The School of Business seeks a conscientious, reliable student to work as a general administrative assistant. Duties include, but are not limited to, assisting the Dean's office with a variety of projects, assist faculty and staff as required. The student will be expected to perform a wide range of administrative tasks and support of the educational mission of the School of Business.

Duties
Administrative and operational support for professors, staff and visitors.
Keep bulletin boards updated.
Sort and distribute mail, packages and faxes.
Oversee mailroom, kitchen and lounge area; keep neat and clean.
Keep logbook with sign-out sheet for all equipment, cables, etc.
Assistance with A/V setup in conference rooms.
Keep all equipment in operational condition.
Courier to Howe Center and around campus as needed.
Special projects (mailings, printing and binding, copying) as needed.
Maintain water coolers and office equipment on 4th and 6th floors.
Maintain inventory for office supplies.

Skills:
Strong Microsoft Office skills
Strong Mac OS X Skills are a plus
Professional demeanor
Ability to communicate
Ability to self-organize / solve problems

Interested applicants for this position should submit a cover letter and resume.

Department
College Reception Area
