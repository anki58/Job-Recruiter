Job Description
This position will offer support with general office tasks, as well as assist with the front desk at the Office of Graduate Admissions. Hourly position - 20hrs/week.

Duties
Assist in the daily operations of the Office of Graduate Admissions. This may include supporting administrative activities, running errands as well as data entry, data collection and processing,
Support the front desk as needed by providing general information to students in person or through telephone calls.
Assist in updating the graduate admissions website.
Be able and willing to communicate with prospective students.
Assist in running reports using pivot tables and provide data analysis
Assist in maintaining the admissions blog, and social media portals.
Assist staff with IT issues as needed
Support graduate open houses
Other duties as assigned
Qualifications
Ability to operate office machines, such as photocopiers and scanners, facsimile machines.
Be very punctual and willing to work a schedule 5 days a week - Mon-Fri; follow priorities, organize workload, handle multiple responsibilities and meet deadlines.
Must be physically able to lift 40 lbs
Must have excellent communication (oral and written) skills. A quick learner, detail oriented with strong interpersonal skills and demonstrate the ability to work independently and as part of a team.
Experience using social media professionally a plus.
Experience and proficiency using Microsoft Office 2003, 2007, and 2010 (Excel, Access, PowerPoint, Word, Outlook). Must be an advanced user of excel – experience working with pivot tables.
Must be able to learn other software tools required by the department.
Proficient in Internet related applications such as E-Mail clients (Outlook, Mac Mail, and Thunder Bird) FTP clients and Web Browsers.
Experience with Photoshop, Flash and Dreamweaver is a plus. Some experience developing web graphics for web sites, Flash and multi-media assets (not necessary but would be a plus)
Ability to troubleshoot Windows and Macintosh Operating Systems.
Appropriate technical support experience and knowledge of multiple internet browsers
Must have started classes at Stevens in the Fall 2016 semester.
The position is open only to graduate students.
